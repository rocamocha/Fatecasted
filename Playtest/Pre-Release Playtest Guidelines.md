# Player IDs
Each playtester will be assigned a unique Player ID, consisting of 5 alphanumeric characters.
> [!abstract] Example Player IDs
> A79B1, G6H33, 77B9J

> [!caution]
> Be careful to avoid any combinations that can be read as a word or phrase that is offensive.
# Gathering Feedback
Playtest feedback will be gathered via [Typeform](https://typeform.com)
