While the process described in [[Limited Affiliation & Revenue Share]] has a lot of good things, it also carries a lot of inherent risks based on unknown factors.

One of these risks is the over-personalization of gameplay objects, which could make gameplay confusing depending on how certain players interpret things visually and functionally.
>[!caution] In other words...
>...we could end up creating a situation where it commonly feels like everyone has "house rules" just based on visual representation from customization! That would be bad!

# A certain degree of control...
In order to avoid this problem, we need to employ some sort of control over the game and community to prevent this problem from emerging - BUT, we also want to avoid [[Release of Documentation#The Authority Problem|the authority problem]].
>[!question] How the hell do you do that?
>Please help.
