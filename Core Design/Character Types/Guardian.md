Guardians focus on enforcing some kind of disadvantage through restriction or conditions. They should feel like they take away a little bit of the opponent's control.
>[!idea] Mechanics that Guardians play around include:
>- [[Suppress]]
>- [[Weaken]]
>- [[Absorb]]
