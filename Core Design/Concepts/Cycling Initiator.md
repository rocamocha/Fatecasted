The game is played in "rounds." Each round is a single cycle of the above timeline. After each round, the player numbering cycles by 1 (the last player is now the first and the first is last in a duel). The first player in the cycle is the initiator for that round.

>[!caution] The cycling initiator is designed to...
>- ...provide a position of advantage and disadvantage to each player on a rotating basis.
>- ...act as a vehicle to make commitments such as stacking or seeding into fatelines impactful and meaningful.
>
