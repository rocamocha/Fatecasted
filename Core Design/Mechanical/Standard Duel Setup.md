## Getting Started
The Fatecasted *Standard Duel* format is an epic battle that takes place between 2 players. Before commencing play for this format, both players should have prepared the following prior:
> [!success] A selection of at least 5 (and no more than 9) [[Character Cards]], of which...
>1.  ...no other card in the set matches in *Title* on the **front** side.
>2. ...no other card in the set matches in *Type* on the **front** side.
>3. ...the sum of all cards' **rarity** does not exceed 21.

> [!success] A selection of exactly 11 [[Fate Cards]], of which...
>1. ...there are no more than 3 copies of any individual card.
>2. ...there are no more than 5 memories.
>3. ...each memory is unique in Title.

> [!success] Any tokens or markers that may be required for play, which may include those needed to represent:
> 1. [[Tokens#Damage|Damage Assignments]]
> 2. Reinforcement
> 3. [[Crystal Charges]]
