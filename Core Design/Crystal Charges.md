Crystal charges are a versatile resources that each player gains a single instance of, on every turn. Their usage is varied, and the main function is to provide a way to limit certain actions to a resource tied to time. Besides this, they can also be used as the prerequisite for certain global mechanics such as Realizing Destiny.

When a crystal charge is gained, it cannot be used on that same turn.

>Crystal charges should be represented by a token with a binary indicator such as a coin with two sides, in order to make tracking the state of the crystal charge easier.

Each player can have a maximum of 12 crystal charges at once.
