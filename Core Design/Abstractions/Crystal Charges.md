Crystal charges are a versatile resources that players can gain in a variety of ways. Their usage is varied, and the main function is to provide a way to limit certain actions to a resource tied to time or action. Besides this, they can also be used as the prerequisite for certain global mechanics such as Realizing Destiny.

Each player can have a maximum of 12 crystal charges at once.
