# Fatecasted

Welcome to the official repository for Fatecasted, a strategy tabletop card game where player control and community creativity take center stage.

## What is Fatecasted?

Fatecasted is a tabletop strategy card game for ages 12 and up. It's designed from the ground up to answer one question: "Should the player have more control?"

Our answer is a resounding **"Yes."**

In Fatecasted, you won't find random booster packs or a constantly shifting meta that forces you to buy new cards to stay competitive. Instead, you'll find a deep, strategic experience where deliberate choices, resource management, and creative thinking are the keys to victory.

---

### For Players: A New Kind of Card Game

Are you tired of games where victory is decided by a lucky draw? Fatecasted replaces the random "draw phase" of traditional card games with a **Summon Phase**, where both players build their teams with open-ended, deliberate choices.

- **Character Cards:** These are the heart of your team. Each character has a unique type, abilities, and a "Destiny" on the back of the card, offering new strategic options when flipped.
- **Fate Cards:** This is where you express your unique playstyle. By setting cards in your "Fate Area," you introduce unknown variables, creating a tense, psychological battle of wits with your opponent.
- **Deep Strategy:** The game is built on a philosophy of "you can't always be right." Success comes from your ability to analyze the board, anticipate your opponent, and make tough decisions.

Our goal is to create a game that is easy to learn but has a high skill ceiling. A new player will understand the *what* and *how* of an action, while an experienced player will master the *why* and *when*.

### For Creators: A Partnership of Passion

We believe in celebrating the artists who help bring our universe to life. Fatecasted is built on a unique partnership with creators.

- **Limited Edition, Artist-Centric Sets:** Instead of random booster packs, we plan to release limited-run sets of cards featuring commissioned art from talented artists.
- **A True Partnership:** Artists who will collaborate with us should receive a significant portion of the proceeds from their set's sales. We want this to be a partnership that helps everyone grow.
- **Supporting Creativity:** Players collect new sets because they love the artist and want to support their work, not because they need the cards to win. This changes the "new set" dynamic from a competitive necessity to a cosmetic and collectible choice that directly supports the creative community.

This approach allows us to showcase unique artistic styles and give back to the creators who make Fatecasted beautiful.
